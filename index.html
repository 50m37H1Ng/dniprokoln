a research of cultural initiatives of Dnipro and the region


about the project

Kultura Medialina, a non-governmental organization, is launching a research project to study cultural organizations in the city if Dnipro and the region. One of the aims of this project is to find out how different cultural institutions work during a full-scale war and to facilitate their cooperation with other European organizations.

Currently, Kultura Medialna (Ukraine) together with Ensemble platform and association Freihandelszone (Germany) is working on creating a network of cooperation between the initiatives of Dnipro and Cologne, that are going to become sister cities in 2022. For a year now, Dnipro has been receiving humanitarian aid as part of the short-term goals of cooperation between the cities. Understanding the importance of a dialogue between organizations from different countries, Kultura Medialna and Freihandelszone strive to expand this cooperation to the fields of culture, art and education. The geography of the project will cover the Dnipropetrovsk region and the land of North Rhine-Westphalia.

During the first stage of this project, we are planning to conduct a research to find out the current needs and the state of local cultural organizations. Various public associations, initiatives, communal enterprises that are continuing their activity in Dnipro or the region during the full-scale war, and continue to work actively in the field of culture, art, urbanism, work with communities, etc., are eligible to take part in this project. These could be museums, theaters, public organizations, art and cultural centers, galleries, festivals, concert spaces, educational institutions in the field of culture and art.
